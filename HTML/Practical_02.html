What is Internet ?

The Internet is a global network that connects millions of computers and devices around the world. It allows people to share information, communicate, and access a vast amount of data instantly. Through the internet, users can browse websites, send emails, watch videos, use social media, and much more. It has become an essential part of modern life, making communication faster and learning easier. The internet has transformed how we work, study, shop, and stay connected with others across the globe.
