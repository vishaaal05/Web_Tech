Holi

Holi is one of the most vibrant and joyful festivals celebrated in India. It is known as the Festival of Colors and marks the arrival of spring. People celebrate Holi by playing with colors, dancing to music, and sharing sweets with friends and family. It is a time when people forget past differences and come together in harmony.
